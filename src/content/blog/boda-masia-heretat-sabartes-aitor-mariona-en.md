Weddings are moments full of emotion, beauty and unforgettable memories. When Aitor and Mariona chose how to celebrate their union, they picked two stunning venues in Tarragona that perfectly captured the essence of their story: **Masia Heretat Sabartés** and the **Santuari de Montferri**. In this post we explore the charm of each one and how they came together to create a magical day.

## The charm of Masia Heretat Sabartés

Masia Heretat Sabartés is a traditional Catalan farmhouse with a warm, rustic atmosphere, ideal for couples looking for a blend of history and natural beauty. Tucked away in the Tarragona countryside, the venue offers a peaceful setting surrounded by vineyards and olive groves.

The architecture of the masia is one of its highlights: stone walls, wooden beams and spacious terraces create an inviting environment that feels both elegant and intimate. For Aitor and Mariona, this setting allowed their guests to relax and enjoy the celebration in a sophisticated but welcoming space.

![Masia Heretat Sabartés](/blog/boda-masia-heretat-sabartes-aitor-mariona/2d4f94_568ce8b8094c4e8cb27717cf73ed5dad~mv2.jpg)

### What makes Masia Heretat Sabartés special

- **Outdoor spaces**: the large gardens and terraces are perfect for cocktail hours and group photos.
- **Versatile interiors**: the rooms adapt to dining, dancing or quieter moments.
- **Local cuisine**: the catering includes options that showcase traditional Catalan flavours, adding authenticity to the whole experience.

This combination of natural beauty and cultural richness made the masia an ideal choice for the reception, where family and friends gathered to celebrate the couple's new chapter.

## The spiritual setting of Santuari de Montferri

The ceremony took place at the **Santuari de Montferri**, a unique sanctuary perched on a hill with panoramic views over the Tarragona region. This church is known for its distinctive architecture and serene atmosphere, a spiritual backdrop that adds depth to any wedding.

The sanctuary's design blends Gothic and modern elements, with tall arches and stained-glass windows that fill the interior with coloured light. For Aitor and Mariona, exchanging vows here was a meaningful experience that connected their love to a place charged with history and faith.

### Highlights of the Santuari de Montferri

- **Scenic location**: the hilltop position offers breathtaking views, perfect for memorable wedding photos.
- **Architectural beauty**: the mix of styles creates a distinctive and inspiring space.
- **Peaceful ambience**: the quiet, solemn feel of the sanctuary reinforces the weight of the ceremony.

Choosing this venue added a layer of tradition and spirituality to the day, making the moment even more special for the couple and their guests.

## Making two venues work in a single day

Combining two locations can be a challenge, but Aitor and Mariona's wedding flowed smoothly thanks to careful planning. The transition from the ceremony at the sanctuary to the celebration at Masia Heretat Sabartés was seamless.

### Tips if you're planning to use two venues

- **Plan transport**: arrange clear, comfortable transport for guests between the two sites.
- **Coordinate timing**: leave room to travel and settle in without rushing.
- **Communicate clearly**: give guests detailed schedules and directions.
- **Match the styles**: pick venues that complement each other in atmosphere and aesthetics.

This approach meant guests enjoyed every moment without confusion or delays, and the day felt like one cohesive celebration.

Aitor and Mariona personalised their wedding with details that reflected their personalities and shared history. From floral arrangements inspired by local flora to a music selection with traditional Catalan songs, every element added to a celebration with real meaning.

They also built in moments that encouraged guests to interact: a wine tasting featuring local wineries, and a photo booth with props tied to their hobbies. These touches created a warm, joyful atmosphere that guests still talk about.

## Why Masia Heretat Sabartés and Santuari de Montferri are ideal for weddings

For couples looking for a wedding that combines natural beauty, cultural richness and spiritual depth, these two venues are a perfect match. The masia offers a welcoming, elegant space for the celebration; the sanctuary, a striking and meaningful setting for the ceremony.

Together, they create a wedding experience that balances tradition and modernity, intimacy and grandeur, nature and architecture.
