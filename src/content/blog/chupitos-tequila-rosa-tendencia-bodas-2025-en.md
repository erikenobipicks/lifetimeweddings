### Pink Tequila shots: the craziest trend of 2025

If you're planning your wedding and want to surprise your guests with something **fun, unexpected and guaranteed to spark laughs from the first minute**, write this down: **pink Tequila shots on every table!** 🍓🥃

Yes, more and more couples are dropping **mini Tequila Rose bottles** on the tables… and what looked like a small detail ends up being **one of the biggest moments of the wedding**.

From the spontaneous toast to that guest who goes full brave and drinks straight from the bottle while everyone cheers, **this trend is blowing up** at 2025 weddings.

![Pink Tequila shots: the craziest trend](/blog/chupitos-tequila-rosa-tendencia-bodas-2025/2d4f94_015ac072bcd14dd6bacb10b0f4f8bfb4~mv2.jpg)

### 🧑‍✈️ BONUS: pick a "table captain" to kick things off

Want to take it to the next level? Appoint a "**table captain**" at each table: the funniest guest, the most social one, or frankly the most dangerous with a bottle in hand 😜

Give them a sticker, a sash or a little sign to identify them… and **make them in charge of pouring the shots and rallying the table**. Works every time: they become the spiritual leader of the group for the night.

### 📸 Why does it work so well?

- It's **photogenic and viral** (the pink colour and the scene kill it in reels).
- It breaks the ice and **gets guests interacting**.
- It sparks **genuine reactions** that look great on video.
- It reinforces the "fun wedding" vibe without resorting to clichés.

At Lifetime Weddings we've already captured a few of these scenes… and we promise, they're pure gold! 🎥✨

### 💡 Where to place them?

- On each table, with a little sign: "Only for the brave 🍓".
- On a "roaming shot cart" that moves around the room.
- As a surprise drop during the party, just before the first dance.

And if you pair it with some **party glasses**, **lights** and a **DJ dropping the banger of the night…** *BOOM: epic wedding guaranteed.*
