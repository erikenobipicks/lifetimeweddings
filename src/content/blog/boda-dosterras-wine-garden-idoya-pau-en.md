### Idoya and Pau at their wedding at Dosterras Wine Garden: a celebration among vineyards and emotions

![Dosterras Wine Garden: a celebration among vineyards and emotions](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_d6db55549bc4487a9ce0153cc6d7b4a7~mv2.webp)

*Dosterras Wine Garden (Marçà).*

### Getting ready in a dreamlike setting

On October 12, Idoya and Pau chose **Dosterras Wine Garden** in Marçà as the perfect setting for their wedding. Surrounded by vineyards and with imposing views over the **Serra de Llaberia Nature Reserve**, the venue offered a calm, magical atmosphere for their big day. The 19th-century masia and the large gardens provided ideal spaces for every moment of the celebration.

![Getting ready at Dosterras Wine Garden](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_2cd87019be8c45789e54c9f84053c246~mv2.webp)

Getting ready started with Pau, followed by Idoya. Both of them got ready in rooms with mirrors that let us capture natural, spontaneous moments, faithful to our photography style that always looks for **good light and composition**. Naturalness and authenticity were front and centre from the start.

### Idoya's dress: elegance by Otaduy

Idoya dazzled in a [**spectacular dress by Otaduy**](https://otaduy.es/), a Spanish label known for artisanal, modern designs that reflect each bride's unique personality. The dress, with clean lines and delicate details, heightened Idoya's elegance and style, making her the centre of attention.

![Bridal dress: elegance by Otaduy](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_e49bbb5d73fd4ff1b37d08aae17b4b1f~mv2.webp)

*A beautiful bridal dress by Otaduy.*

### Intimate, emotional ceremony

The couple went for an **unconventional ceremony**, improvising words for their family and friends, as well as personal vows for each other. This relaxed, spontaneous approach created an intimate, emotional atmosphere, where even some guests felt moved to share a few words, adding a special touch to the moment.

The on-and-off rain kept us company but, far from being a problem, it brought freshness to a warm day and added a romantic note to the atmosphere.

![Ceremony at Dosterras Wine Garden](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_337e6fa1c9db4ffeb94aba5c031682a4~mv2.webp)

### Couple session among the vineyards: brief but meaningful

After the ceremony we did a brief newlywed session around the estate. The **picturesque corners and panoramic views** of the venue were perfect backdrops to capture Idoya and Pau's essence. That said, the couple was eager to join the party and celebrate with their loved ones, so the session stayed short but full of real moments.

![Wedding at Dosterras](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_45f4464e8ce34814a14d7516a97fb162~mv2.webp)

### Reception and party: overflowing joy

The guests, already enjoying the catering by **El Mallol**, welcomed the couple with huge energy as they walked in with **smoke flares**, marking the start of a vibrant celebration. During the cocktail hour, **DjDisccontrol**'s music kept everyone dancing and enjoying, creating endless fun moments we captured on photo and video.

The night continued with a flamenco band that had everyone singing and dancing non-stop, filling the air with energy and joy.

![Party at Dosterras with live music](/blog/boda-dosterras-wine-garden-idoya-pau/2d4f94_78e7fb637006455fb160e0d03e8ba47e~mv2.webp)

### An unforgettable day at Dosterras Wine Garden

Idoya and Pau's wedding was an intense, emotional celebration. We loved working at **Dosterras Wine Garden**, a venue that pairs natural beauty with top-tier facilities, ideal for memorable events. The warmth and joy of the guests turned this day into an unforgettable experience, and we think the photos capture the essence of what we lived perfectly.
