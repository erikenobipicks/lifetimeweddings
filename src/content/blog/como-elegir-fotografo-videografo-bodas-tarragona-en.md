## 1️⃣ Wedding photography and video aren't an extra — they're a lifelong investment

Your wedding day **flies by**. While you enjoy it with family and friends, someone has to focus on capturing **the emotions, the spontaneous moments, every small detail that will let you live it again and again**.

💡 **Think about it:** ❌ Memories fade with time. ✅ Your wedding photos and video let you relive that day forever.

📷 **The key is choosing professionals who know how to capture real emotion, without forcing poses or artificial moments.**

## 2️⃣ Why is it better to choose a photo and video team that always works together?

One of the biggest mistakes many couples make is **hiring a photographer and a videographer who don't know each other and haven't worked together before**.

🚨 **What many photographers and videographers don't tell you** 🚨 There are studios that **sell you the idea of a photo and video team**, but in reality **the photographer and the videographer have never met before the wedding day**.

- **Sometimes they don't even know who will cover the event until a few days before.**
- **They can subcontract whoever is available at the moment, regardless of whether their style and way of working match.**
- **There's no real connection between them, which can affect the flow of the coverage.**

📌 **The result?** Coverage where photo and video don't fit together because **there's no chemistry, no coordination and no shared vision**.
