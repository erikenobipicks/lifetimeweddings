When you dream about your wedding day, you picture moments full of emotion, love and magic. And without a doubt, what will stay forever are the photographs. 💕 They're what, years from now, will transport you back to every moment you lived.

That's why today I want to share **the 10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot**. The images no bride should let slip by because they capture the essence of that great day. If you want an album that tells your love story in an authentic and moving way, take notes.

### 1️⃣ **The dance moment (or a photo that suggests it)**

The first dance as a married couple is one of the most emotional and romantic moments of the wedding day. But even if that moment hasn't arrived yet, a photo that evokes it during the couple's session can be just as magical. 💫 A soft spin, a pose that hints at a waltz, or simply an embrace with movement create images that convey closeness and love. This kind of photo is perfect for opening or closing your wedding album, and it's a favourite with every bride.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_6359ca6104724739a67bbe3e64691161~mv2.jpg)

### 2️⃣ **Hands with the rings**

Among all the photos that can't be missing from your wedding, the detail of your hands intertwined showing the wedding bands holds a special place. 💍 It's an image that symbolises your commitment and the bond you sealed that day. You can take it right after the ceremony, during the couple's session, or even during the getting-ready. The important thing is for the photographer to catch the emotion you feel looking at those hands and remembering that you're now husband and wife.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_d5990e78dd6d4a6e87677aa331c2f418~mv2.jpg)

### 3️⃣ **The bride's bouquet in close-up**

The bouquet is one of the most personal and carefully chosen elements of the whole bridal look. 🌸 That's why it deserves its starring moment. A photo of the bouquet in your hands, resting somewhere special or even while you walk, adds a touch of colour and romance to the coverage. This image is also ideal to highlight the small details you chose with so much love.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_eb0cbc0d8e7848878458293cbe004b14~mv2.jpg)

### 4️⃣ **The kiss… or that "almost kiss" full of emotion**

The newlyweds' kiss is THE quintessential photo. But sometimes the instant right before the kiss — when your eyes meet and you can feel the emotion in the air — is even more special. That "almost kiss" image conveys tenderness, longing and love, and is one of the must-have photos of your wedding shoot. 💞 Ask your photographer to stay alert to those magical seconds that happen on their own.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_7956875923204ed6b6de6d3e4a9079ae~mv2.jpg)

### 5️⃣ **Walking together: the transition shot that tells a story**

One of the most beautiful photos in any wedding coverage is the one where you walk together. From behind, holding hands, or moving towards the camera — this kind of image brings dynamism and a sense of journey to your album. 🌿 It's perfect to symbolise the start of the path you'll walk together as a couple. It also gives the coverage a very natural, relaxed touch that every bride tends to adore.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_a9e80c2cf24a4208b013ee21286c00f9~mv2.jpg)

### 6️⃣ **The hug that says it all**

That hug where you forget about the world and only the two of you exist. The image that, when you look at it, brings back the warmth, the emotion and the security of that moment. 🤍 A heartfelt hug is one of the photos that can't be missing from your wedding because it speaks of real love — the kind you feel and truly live. Find a beautiful corner, skip the poses, and let the moment come on its own.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_37eee7a520a544d5a33b98346d768986~mv2.jpg)

### 7️⃣ **The gazes that make you fall in love**

Gazes are a window to the soul, and at a wedding yours shine in a special way. 🌟 A photo of the two of you looking at each other with love, with complicity, with that mix of nerves and happiness, is a memory for life. This kind of image is the one that moves you most when you come back to it years later. Have your photographer catch those seconds in which you tell each other everything without a single word.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_bad367cc133b48ec892748c1ce4019ab~mv2.jpg)

### 8️⃣ **The couple's portrait: an image to frame**

Among the must-have wedding photos, a portrait of the two of you together can't be missing — a carefully crafted image that highlights the best of you as a couple. ✨ It can be a classic portrait, more posed, or something more natural, playing with the setting, the light and the gestures. This is the kind of photo many brides choose to print and hang at home, because it reflects the essence of who you are on that important day.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_4bf6d69303eb43efb5d0449a93727c71~mv2.jpg)

### 9️⃣ **The veil in the wind (if you wear one)**

If you've chosen to wear a veil, don't miss the chance to have a photo with it in the wind. 💫 The movement of the veil brings an ethereal, almost magical touch to the images. Your photographer will know how to make the most of it: a breath of natural air, a light movement of your hands, or even with the help of an assistant. The result: photos that look straight out of a fairytale.

![10 must-have photos from your wedding shoot](/blog/10-fotos-imprescindibles-sesion-boda/2d4f94_891418fab3664b00b8a9817ad74e1e81~mv2.jpg)

### 🔟 **A fun or spontaneous photo: the one you'll always remember**

No matter how planned everything is, the moments that appear out of nowhere are the ones you'll remember most. A real laugh, a silly gesture, an unexpected dance… That photo that captures your natural self and makes you relive the moment over and over. 💕 Spontaneous photos are the ones that convey the most and the ones that best tell the story of how your big day really was.
