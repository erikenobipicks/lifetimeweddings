### 2. David Griso

[David Griso](https://davidgriso.com/) is an unmissable reference in wedding photography. His style is distinct and recognisable: naturalness, warmth and raw emotion. He has the ability to connect with couples from the first minute, and it shows in every photo. For us, one of the great names in Catalonia and across Spain.

### 3. Robert Marcillas

[Robert](https://robertmarcillas.com/) is one of the most recognised photographers nationally. His style is elegant, modern and deeply emotional. With a strong social media presence and a loyal community, he has found couples who want something more than a standard coverage — they want an experience. His images stand out for their careful editing, the connection with the couple and a very strong visual narrative.

### 4. Marc Prades

[Marc](https://marc-prades.com/) is a photographer with a special sensitivity for capturing real emotion. With a long career and multiple international awards, he stands out for his documentary, natural style. His photos have been recognised on platforms like Inspirations Photographers, Flecha en Blanco and FdB, and one of his images appeared on the cover of Javier Cercas' "Terra Alta" (Premio Planeta). ### 5. Estudi de Llum

[Adrià](https://www.estudidellum.com/), based in Vila-seca (Tarragona), offers through Estudi de Llum a natural, documentary, spontaneous style. ### 6. Joy Zamora

[Joy Zamora](https://joyzamora.com/) is a photographer who works at a world-class level but often shoots in Catalonia. His style is intimate, artistic and deeply emotional. Beyond the visual talent, he stands out for his ability to connect with people and tell authentic stories. A different take that has inspired many professionals in the sector.

### 7. Yasmina & Víctor Fotografía

From Les Terres de l'Ebre, [Yasmina and Víctor](https://yasminavictor.com/) are a photographer couple with a sincere, close, natural eye. Friends and colleagues of ours, on some days we're lucky enough to share work with them and we know first-hand that they put their soul into every shoot. Their style is documentary, emotional and with a sensitivity that shows in every image.

### How do you choose the best photographer for your wedding?

The most important thing is that you connect with their style and their way of working. If you need help, here's a useful [guide on how to choose the right photographer for your wedding](/en/blog/como-elegir-fotografo-videografo-bodas-tarragona) that can help you make a better decision. Check their social media, ask for complete albums (important that they show you real weddings, not just editorial sessions!), and above all, talk to them before deciding.

A key point is that the photographer should be active on social, posting recent work and quality content. That reflects commitment and passion for the craft.
